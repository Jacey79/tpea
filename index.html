Evidence sources
VP

Validated perturbation

Causal evidence from KO, KD, overexpression, or pharmacological intervention producing measurable changes in pain behavior.

VDE

Validated differential expression

Hypothesis-driven RNA or protein-level confirmation of differential abundance using targeted assays (RT-qPCR, Western blot, IHC, IF, ELISA).

HTP

High-throughput profiling

Hypothesis-free multi-gene differential expression from bulk RNA-seq, miRNA-seq, or microarray comparing pain vs. control conditions.
